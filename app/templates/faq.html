My preferred platform is not listed, why?

To avoid unecessarily cluttering the website, we list platforms up to the PS3 / Xbox 360 / Wii era, as those were the first generation of consoles to make use of online features for a lot of games. Internet connectivity might have existed in older games but microtransactions were not really a concern yet.
